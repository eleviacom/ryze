# Ryze — 60-Second Product Film · Script

**Tone:** Apple/Linear product film. Confident, warm, a little cheeky. Music: upbeat electronic / lo-fi house, ~120 BPM — cuts land on the downbeats.
**VO voice:** young, smooth, Gen‑Z-credible (EN). ~155 words → ~60s with breathing room.

---

## A) Voiceover read (paste into ElevenLabs as-is)

> For your whole life, banking was built for someone else.
> Not anymore.
>
> This is Ryze.
>
> Open a real Raiffeisen account in two minutes — your phone, a photo, done. Fully online.
>
> Everything in one place: your balance, your goals, your level.
>
> Meet Riz — your AI money coach. Ask it *"how am I spending this month?"* and get a real answer, from your real data.
>
> Send, request, and split with your crew. Money that moves like a group chat.
>
> Virtual and physical cards — freeze them, set your limits, design your own.
>
> Round up your spare change, set goals, and watch your money grow.
>
> And every smart move earns. Streaks, missions, real rewards.
> Play. Invite. Belong.
>
> Ryze. Money that levels up with you.
