## 4. Description (max 5000, markdown) — paste as-is
### What is Ryze?
Ryze is a gamified mobile bank for young Albanians (14–25), built on real Raiffeisen rails. It turns the intimidating "first bank account" into something Gen-Z actually wants to open, use, and stick with — by wrapping everyday banking in an AI money coach, social payments, savings goals, and a rewards game.

### The problem
For most young people a first bank account is paperwork, not empowerment. Traditional banking apps are built for adults, in adult language, with zero guidance and zero reason to build good habits early. The result is low financial literacy, low engagement, and a generation that learns money the hard way.

### The solution
Ryze keeps **real banking** (accounts, cards, payments, KYC) but makes it feel like the apps young people already love:

- **2-minute onboarding** — fully online KYC (phone, OTP, ID scan, face check), Albania-correct legal disclosures, hard 18+ age handling, EN + Albanian.
- **Riz, the AI money coach** — ask *"How am I spending this month?"* and get a real answer from your actual data, with input-safety guardrails and an offline fallback so it works even without an API key.
- **Money that's social** — send and request like texting (chat threads with money bubbles), and split bills with your crew in seconds.
- **Cards** — virtual + physical, freeze & limits, and a Card Studio to personalise colour and print your own text.
- **Grow** — savings goals with round-ups, plus spending analytics.
- **A rewards game (Play · Invite · Belong)** — daily streaks with a multiplier, levels/tiers (Rookie → Saver → Pro → Elite), AI-generated missions tied to *real money actions*, a squad goal, invite-to-earn, and a points store with real coupons (Spotify, KFC, …) redeemed via QR.

### Why it fits "Play, Invite, Belong"
Every mechanic maps directly: **Play** (streaks, levels, missions), **Invite** (referrals, squad goal), **Belong** (identity, rewards, community). Crucially, points are earned through *good financial behaviour*, so engagement and financial literacy rise together.

### Tech
Native **SwiftUI** (iOS 17+) with a clean modular architecture (Bank, Game, Onboarding and Riz models) that maps 1:1 to the challenge's logical services. Riz runs on a live backend with a safe offline local fallback. Fully localised EN/Albanian, real Albanian Lek with ALL↔EUR exchange, adaptive light/dark, and a Revolut-grade design system built around a single scarce Raiffeisen "Banana Yellow" stamp (see `BRANDING.md`).

### What's next
Real Raiffeisen API integration, biometric step-up on payments, push notifications, and a parent/guardian view for under-18s.
